Synergy Squad

Synergy Squad is a team that has worked together since first year. We worked in a year module where we built a culture of open communication, quality work, a network of support, and great conflict resolution.

Ultimately what brought us together for INF 370 was a shared vision that strives for excellence, our trust in one another to produce high quality work and after, the core four, placed in the top 5 of our cohort for the INF 271 group project with an average of 83.63% it was a no brainer to come together.

And we brought in a fifth member who we've worked before who is part of the top 15% of the EBIT faculty and a Golden Key member.
