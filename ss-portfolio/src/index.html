Synergy Squad

Synergy Squad is a team that has worked together since our first year of university, where we discovered that our strongest advantage was our ability to work seamlessly together.

Our name reflects the synergy created through clear communication, mutual accountability, and a shared commitment to delivering high-quality information systems

Guided by an ethos of excellence, reliability, and continuous improvement, we approach every project with a systems-thinking mindset.

This approach led to us securing BMW ZA IT Hub as our industry partner for the INF 370 Capstone Project.

This partnership reflects the strength of our teamwork, problem-solving approach, and technical capabilities, and provides us with the opportunity to work on real-world challenges within a globally recognised organisation.
